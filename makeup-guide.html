Step 01

Skin Prep —
the real foundation

Great makeup starts before the first product touches your face. Your skincare routine is the canvas everything else builds on.

01

Double Cleanse

Start with an oil-based cleanser to dissolve sunscreen & makeup, followed by a water-based cleanser. This two-step method ensures your skin is truly clean without stripping its natural barrier. Try the DHC Deep Cleansing Oil + CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser combo.

02

Exfoliate (2–3x/week)

Chemical exfoliants like AHAs (glycolic, lactic acid) smooth texture and brighten. BHAs (salicylic acid) clear pores. Never scrub dry skin — exfoliate after cleansing on damp skin. The Paula's Choice 2% BHA is a cult favourite for 20s skin.

03

Hydrating Toner / Essence

Pat (don't rub) a hydrating toner into skin to prep for serum absorption. Look for ingredients like hyaluronic acid, niacinamide, and centella asiatica. The COSRX Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence is a bestseller for plumped, dewy skin.

04

Vitamin C Serum (AM)

In your 20s, start using Vitamin C every morning. It protects from free radical damage, fades pigmentation, and creates an inner glow that makeup can't replicate. Skinceuticals CE Ferulic is the gold standard; The Ordinary Vitamin C Suspension is budget-friendly.

05

Moisturize + SPF 50

Seal everything in with a moisturizer suited to your skin type, then apply SPF 50+ as the last skincare step. This is non-negotiable in your 20s — it's the single best anti-ageing investment you can make. Try the Supergoop Unseen Sunscreen SPF 40 as a makeup primer too.

Pro Secrets

Tips Every 20-Something
Needs to Know

🌊
Damp Beauty Blender Always
Wet your beauty blender until it swells, then squeeze out the water. A damp blender blends foundation seamlessly and prevents it from absorbing into the sponge. Dry blenders absorb half your product.
🔄
Color Correct Before Concealing
For dark under-eye circles, use a peach or orange corrector first (darker skin tones need deeper orange). This cancels purple tones so your concealer doesn't have to work as hard — the result looks more natural and lasts longer.
❄️
Bake Your Concealer
Apply loose powder over concealer generously, let it sit for 5–10 minutes while you do your eye makeup, then dust away. This technique sets concealer flawlessly and prevents creasing all day.
✂️
Mascara Hack: Use a Spoolie
Before mascara dries, comb through lashes with a clean spoolie to separate clumps. This gives that "individual lash" look that tubing mascaras try to replicate.
💡
Eyeshadow First, Then Foundation
Do your eye look first. Any fallout can be easily wiped off. Then apply your base. This is a makeup artist trick that saves you from ruining a perfect base with powdery fallout.
🌡️
Cream Before Powder Always
Layer cream products together, set with powder, then add powder products on top. Cream over powder looks cakey and doesn't blend. Cream blush under powder bronzer = flawless every time.
💄
Liner to Correct Lipstick Mistakes
Use a concealer or nude lip liner around the edges of your lips to clean up any mistakes and sharpen your lip line. This also stops feathering with dark lipsticks — a pro trick from every backstage.
🕰️
Start Retinol in Your Mid-20s
It's never too early to start. Low-strength retinol (0.025–0.05%) used 2x/week at night boosts collagen, speeds cell turnover, and prevents the fine lines that show up in your 30s. Always use SPF the next day.
